To give your PvP Mob a name need to farm stones to drop some items :
- Passion Flower
- Ancient Parchment
When you have all necessary items go to the Tamer and open the Crafting Panel. There put this tow items and craft the "Bottle of Nameless"

With "Bottle of Nameless" you are able to give your PvP Mob a name. Be careful what name you give him because you are no longer to change it. When you use this potion he ask twice what its the name you want to give to your PvP Mob.
